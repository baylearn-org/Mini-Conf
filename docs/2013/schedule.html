Schedule

The event starts at 9:30am and ends at 6pm on Wednesday, August 28th, 2013. Registration opens at 9:00am.
 
The program includes
  • 3 invited talks by Michael Jordan, Trevor Hastie and Yoram Singer;
  • 6 short talks and a poster session;
  • and, most importantly, break/lunch time for discussion.
